Bridging Art and Livelihoods

Madhubani art is more than just a tradition—it’s a story of resilience and creativity. We train women to create exquisite hand-painted designs on bridal wear, lifestyle products, and home décor. By connecting these creations with urban and global markets, we ensure that the artform thrives while providing consistent incomes to artisans.

Affordable and Flexible Childcare/h5>

We recognize the need for quality childcare that fits the diverse needs of families. Through low-cost daycare centers and need-based childcare services, we’re addressing the double burden faced by women, helping them balance work and family life while fostering better early childhood development.
