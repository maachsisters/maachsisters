Maach Sisters Bridging Tradition and Modernity
Reviving Madhubani Art and Building Supportive Communities for a Better Tomorrow

Through the efforts of Maach Sisters, we are bringing the timeless beauty of Madhubani art back into the spotlight while creating a supportive, inclusive community. Our aim is to provide artists with the resources and platform they need to thrive and make a lasting impact on the world.
