About Us

Maach Sisters is a community-driven initiative focused on reviving Madhubani art while supporting women artisans through sustainable livelihoods. We empower women to create beautiful, hand-painted designs and sell them in urban and global markets. Our mission is to build strong communities while preserving traditional art forms.
